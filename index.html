The SERVICES we provide

Food Hampers

"At OVC Alberta, we are committed to supporting individuals and families in need within our community. Through our food assistance program, we provide essential nourishment to those facing food insecurity. Our dedicated volunteers work tirelessly to assemble and distribute food hampers filled with nutritious items, ensuring that no one goes hungry. These hampers include staple food items such as grains, canned goods, fresh produce, and other essentials. By offering this vital support, we aim to alleviate hunger and provide a sense of relief to those experiencing financial hardship. Through the generosity of our donors and the dedication of our volunteers, we strive to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those in need."

Consultation

"We understand that navigating through challenging times can be overwhelming. That's why we offer consultation services tailored to individuals facing various forms of hardship and suffering. Our experienced consultants provide a safe and supportive environment for individuals to express their concerns and explore potential solutions. Whether you're dealing with mental health issues, emotional distress, relationship difficulties, or other personal challenges, our compassionate team is here to listen, offer guidance, and provide practical strategies to help you cope and overcome obstacles. We believe in empowering individuals to regain control over their lives and move towards a brighter future. With our consultation services, you don't have to face your struggles alone."

Job Opportunities

"We believe in equipping students with the resources and support they need to succeed beyond the classroom. In addition to our core mission of providing assistance to those in need, we also offer valuable career services to help students transition into the workforce. Through partnerships with local businesses and organizations, we facilitate job placement opportunities, internships, and career development workshops. Our dedicated team works closely with students to identify their strengths, explore career interests, and connect them with potential employers. Whether it's resume writing assistance, interview preparation, or networking opportunities, we are committed to empowering students to pursue their career aspirations and achieve professional success. By bridging the gap between education and employment, we strive to create a brighter future for the next generation."
